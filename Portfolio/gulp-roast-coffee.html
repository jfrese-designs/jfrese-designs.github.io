Gulp Roast Coffee

I was presented with a contract to redesign the website for Gulp Roast. They wanted to add some color and product pages. The original site was a WIX.com build and pleasing. However, Gulp Roast now wanted to have a new look with additional pages and the ability to add detail without having to redesign each time. The most notable change is the products page which now leads to new individual pages detailing each blend with price information. The site is no longer a WIX build, but a fully custom html coded website.

Design Proposal

The client wanted an initial design proposal made using a specific color scheme and picture for their About Us page. They also asked for it be a responsive design.
